GET THE EDGE IN YOUR MUSIC AND YOUR LIFE

How can coaching help you?

Coaching gives you tailored, individual support to clarify and achieve your creative, personal and professional goals. As your coach, I will become a trusted friend who wants and expects the best from you and will challenge you to create at your peak.

Over the course of six or twelve months, we'll work together towards creating the music and life you've always dreamed of. Whether you want to write an album, perform live, start a label or simply create the best work you're capable of then I'll be with you every step of your journey.

The techniques I employ are based on the knowledge and strategies I’ve developed from helping producers all over the world and working at the heart of the music industry.

Here are some of the challenges I've helped my clients overcome…

I don't have enough time

Music making can easily slip by the wayside, particularly when you have a full-time job, business or family commitments. We'll develop a strategy to keep you moving forward and create more space in your life for creativity.

I’m stuck in a creative rut

Hit a brick wall in your music or your life? Sometimes it’s impossible to get beyond a 16-bar loop or make anything that sounds professional. We'll uncover what's holding you back and get you to your peak creative flow.

I need help promoting myself

Do you struggle with putting yourself and your music out there? Have you ever felt like your work doesn’t sound good enough, feared rejection or lacked the confidence to broaden your network? I'll help you step out of your comfort zone and create new opportunities in your life.

I don’t have all the skills I need

Would you like to master any new skills such as musicality, theory or mixing and mastering? Together, we'll create a development strategy and I'll have your back all the way to ensure you achieve tangible results.

I'd like a career change

Sometimes we reach a point in our lives when we need a change of direction. Together we can navigate that big shift by overcoming doubts and indecision whilst woking on other aspects of your life such as time, finances and relationships.
