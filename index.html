GET THE EDGE IN YOUR MUSIC AND YOUR LIFE

How can coaching help you?

Coaching gives you tailored, individual support to clarify and achieve your creative, personal and professional goals. As your coach, I will be your trusted friend who wants and expects the best and will challenge and inspire you to create at your peak.

Over the course of six or twelve months, we'll work together towards creating the music and life you've always dreamed of. Whether you want to write an album, perform live, start your own music business or simply create the best work you're capable of then I'll be with you every step of your journey.

The techniques I employ are based on the knowledge and strategies I’ve developed from helping producers all over the world and working at the heart of the music industry.

Here are some of the challenges I've helped my clients overcome…

I don't have enough time

Music making can easily slip by the wayside, particularly when you have a full-time job, business or family commitments. We'll develop a strategy to move you forward to success and create that space in your life for creativity.

I’m stuck in a creative rut

Hit a brick wall in your music or your life? Sometimes it’s impossible to get beyond a 16-bar loop or make anything that sounds professional. We'll uncover what's holding you back to get you fired up and in peak creative flow.

I need help promoting myself

Do you struggle with putting yourself and your music out there? Have you ever felt like your work doesn’t sound good enough, feared rejection or lacked the confidence to broaden your network? I'll help you step safely out of your comfort zone and create new opportunities for your music.

I don’t have all the skills I need

Would you like to master any new skills such as musicality, theory or mixing and mastering? Together, we'll create a strategy to help you grow and I'll be there to ensure you achieve those results.

I'd like a career change

Sometimes we reach a point in our lives when we just need a change of direction. Together we'll navigate that shift so you can do passionate and fullfilling work every day and reconnect with who you really are.

So, How does it work?

You’ll get all the help you need to slice through your creative blocks, be way more productive and achieve your goals…

It's all about you

We reguarly hook up for 45 minutes on Skype at a time convenient to you. These sessions are all about you and your needs as a music maker. I won't waste time talking about myself, every minute will be about how we can help you to overcome your creative blocks, move forward and reach your goals in the shortest possible time.

You're never alone

If you get stuck between sessions, want to check-in with your progress or simply need a high-five then just email whenever you want. I guarantee to repond at least twice a week.

Get more done!

At the end of every session we’ll agree on the next steps to move forward with your music and career. I’ll check-in to see how you’re getting on and we’ll continually review your progress to make sure you’re not going off course.

Stay on track

After each session you’ll get a PDF of the main points covered, including challenges you've flagged, long term goals and short action steps you've decided to commit to. This enables you to refer back when you need reminding what the plan is.
