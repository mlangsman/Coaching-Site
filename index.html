GET THE EDGE IN YOUR MUSIC AND YOUR LIFE

How can creative coaching help you?

Making music is like a rollercoaster.

If you're like most music makers then you juggle a day job to pay the bills with production on the side. You're not alone! Many famous musicians have worked regular jobs well into their careers. Bloc Party's Kele was serving popcorn whilst he was featured in NME, and Ian Curtis of Joy Division clocked in as a civil servant between gigs.

If this wasn't difficult enough, the creative process can throw everything up in the air.

How do you know what to write? How do you find a market for you music? Do your tracks sound good enough and is this even really worth pursuing? ... all these thoughts are enough to make you throw in the towel, especially in those dark periods where nothing seems to come our right - if at all.

No matter what level you're at, the creative path is tough and lonely.

But you don't have to face it on your own! Coaching gives you personal support to breakthrough all these blocks, get clarity and achieve your goals.

As your coach, I will become your trusted friend who wants and expects the best and will challenge and inspire you to create at your peak. The techniques I use are based on the knowledge and strategies I’ve developed from helping producers all over the world and working at the heart of the music industry.

Here are some of the challenges I've helped clients overcome…

I need help finishing music

Keep hitting brick walls in your production? Sometimes it’s impossible to get beyond that 16-bar loop or make anything that sounds polished. I'll help you develop the skills, strategies and mindset to finish tracks like a boss.

I don't have enough time

Music making can easily slip by the wayside, particularly when you have a full-time job, business or family commitments. We'll develop a strategy to move you forward to success and create that space in your life for creativity.

I need help promoting myself

Do you struggle with putting yourself and your music out there? Have you ever felt like your work doesn’t sound good enough, feared rejection or lacked the confidence to broaden your network? I'll help you step safely out of your comfort zone and create new opportunities for your music.

I don’t have all the skills I need

Would you like to master any new skills such as musicality, theory or mixing and mastering? Maybe you'd like to be a more original and confident producer or even outsource some parts of your production? I'll help you to upskill and ensure you achieve those results.

I need a career change

Many producers I work with are keen to either get a job in the industry or create their own lifestyle music business. Together we'll explore your options and navigate that shift so you can do passionate and creative work every day and reconnect with who you really are.

How does it work?

You’ll get all the help you need to take your music to the next level...

It's all about you

I coach clients in one-to-one or group sessions. Every minute of these is about helping you to reach your goals in the shortest possible time. We'll cover different topics from finishing music, arrangment and mixing through to how to approach labels, release yourself, get fans and ultimately and earn a living in the industry.

Your bespoke strategy

We'll create a completely personal plan and strategy to get you where you want to be in your life and with your music. At the end of every session we agree on the next steps so you're certain what you need to work on next. Life is always changing so we'll continually review and adjust your plan as we go.

Help and advice on tap

If you're stuck and banging your head against your desk or need some expert advice then email me whenever you want between consultations - I've got your back.

Stay on track

After each session you’ll get a summary of the main points covered, including challenges you've flagged, long term goals and short action steps you've decided to commit to.
