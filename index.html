GET THE EDGE IN YOUR MUSIC AND YOUR LIFE

How can coaching help you?

Coaching gives you tailored, individual support to clarify and achieve your creative, personal and professional goals. As your coach, I will become a trusted friend who wants and expects the best from you and will challenge you to create at your peak.

The techniques I employ are based on the knowledge, distinctions, and strategies I’ve developed from helping hundreds of producers across the world. Here are some of the challenges I’ve helped my clients overcome…

I don't have enough time

Music making can easily slip by the wayside, particularly when you have a full-time job, business or family commitments. This is amplified when we hit creative blocks or face difficult tasks like sending out demos.

I’m stuck in a creative rut

Hit a brick wall in your music or your life? Sometimes things don’t go as we’d like and it’s impossible to get beyond a 16-bar loop or make anything that sounds professional.

I need help promoting myself

Many producers struggle with putting themselves and their music out there. They might feel their work doesn’t sound good enough, fear rejection or lack the skills and confidence to broaden their network.

I don’t have all the skills I need

There are always new ways to learn and grow, whether it’s developing musicality, theory or mixing and mastering. Many producers have trouble deciding which (if any) to focus on, making a commitment and getting tangible results.
